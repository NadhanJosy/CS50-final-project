1. Purpose and Scope

This Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) is designed to assist healthcare professionals in the diagnostic process by providing differential diagnosis suggestions based on symptom patterns. It is intended for educational and decision support purposes only.

2. Not a Medical Device

This system is NOT:

  • A substitute for professional medical judgment
  • A medical device or diagnostic tool
  • Capable of providing final diagnoses
  • Approved for making treatment decisions without clinical verification

3. Clinical Judgment Required

All diagnostic suggestions generated by this system MUST:

  • Be reviewed by qualified healthcare professionals
  • Be verified through appropriate clinical examination
  • Be confirmed with additional diagnostic testing when indicated
  • Consider the complete clinical context

4. Limitations

Users should be aware of the following limitations:

  • The system is trained on a finite dataset and may not recognize rare conditions
  • Accuracy varies based on symptom completeness and quality
  • The system cannot account for all clinical variables
  • Results are probabilistic and not deterministic
  • The system requires accurate symptom input to function properly
